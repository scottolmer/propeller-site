What is Propeller Picks?

Propeller Picks is an AI-assisted player prop research workspace for web and mobile. It scores over/under player props with specialized analysis agents, publishes a public results ledger, and helps users compare prop context before they make their own decisions. Propeller is not a sportsbook and does not accept wagers.

What Propeller Helps With

Propeller helps users research player props by combining matchup context, injury and role changes, historical hit rates, market probability, recent form, and confidence scoring in one workflow.

Who It Is For

Propeller is built for people evaluating pick'em entries and player props on platforms such as Pick6, PrizePicks, Underdog Fantasy, and FanDuel. It is also useful for users who want a faster way to compare prop context before making their own decisions.

What Propeller Is Not

Propeller is not a sportsbook, does not set lines, does not place wagers, and does not pay out entries. It is a research and analytics product.
