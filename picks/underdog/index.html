The Engine Behind the Picks

How Propeller Powers Your Underdog Entries

Underdog Fantasy uses higher/lower picks on player props — the same directional format as Propeller's OVER/UNDER output. Every pick on this page is already formatted for direct Underdog entry use.

OVER/UNDER Maps Directly to Higher/Lower

DIRECT MAPPING

Underdog Fantasy's higher/lower format maps to a standard player-prop direction call: OVER means Higher and UNDER means Lower. The confidence score shows how strongly the available signals support the displayed direction. Always confirm the player, stat, and current Underdog line before making your own entry decision.

Injury Cascade Detection

AVAILABLE SIGNAL

Injury and lineup changes can affect a player's expected role, minutes, shots, or touches. Propeller includes available injury and role signals when relevant, while users should still confirm late news, player status, and the current Underdog projection before making their own decision.

Market Context Comparison

MULTI-BOOK

Available market context can help show whether a listed player-prop line differs from broader pricing. It is one input among the sport-specific signals available for a prop, not a promise of true probability, guaranteed value, or a winning outcome.

Defensive Matchup

POSITION-SPECIFIC

Defense vs Position (DvP) analysis measures how the opponent has defended comparable players at the same position over the season. A dominant wing defender fundamentally changes a volume scorer's Underdog points prop, even when all other signals are bullish. Propeller applies position-specific matchup ratings for every prop.

Usage & Volume Tracking

MULTI-SPORT

For basketball, the Usage agent incorporates BallDontLie tracking data: touches, drives, paint touches, and time of possession. For baseball, the Usage agent models at-bat and plate appearance volume. For hockey, shot attempts and time-on-ice are the key volume signals. Each sport's volume context is calibrated separately.

Hit Rate & Trends

RECENCY-WEIGHTED

Recency-weighted hit rate treats the last 5 games more heavily than the full-season sample. Hot and cold streaks are detected relative to each player's own historical baseline. Streak data is especially relevant for Underdog entries where you are evaluating a specific stat across multiple recent games.
