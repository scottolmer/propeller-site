The Process

How Propeller Generates Picks

Every pick you see here went through the same four-step pipeline before you read it.

01
Data Collection

Odds, injury reports, lineup news, advanced stats, and tracking data are pulled from multiple sources every morning before picks are generated.

02
Sport-Specific Analysis

Available sport-specific signals evaluate listed props using injury, matchup, pace, usage, trend, and no-vig market context when those inputs are available.

03
Confidence Scoring

Available signals combine through sport-specific weights into a displayed direction and model-confidence score. Missing inputs are skipped.

04
Graded & Tracked

After events finish, stored historical analysis rows are graded against actual results and added to the archive. The Results page explains repeats, retrospective data, and both counting units.
