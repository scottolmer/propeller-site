The Process

How Propeller Generates Picks

Every pick you see here went through the same four-step pipeline before you read it.

01
Data Collection

Odds, injury reports, lineup news, advanced stats, and tracking data are pulled from multiple sources every morning before picks are generated.

02
8-Agent Analysis

Eight specialized agents each evaluate every prop independently — injury impact, matchup, pace, usage, trends, no-vig odds, and more.

03
Confidence Scoring

Agent signals combine via calibrated weighted average into a single confidence percentage. Strong agreement among agents produces higher scores.

04
Graded & Tracked

After games finish, every prediction is graded against actual results and added to the public record. No cherry-picking. Ever.
