Features

Built for Pick'em Players Who Want an Edge

One Confidence Score. Eight Reasons Why.

Every prop runs through 8 specialized AI agents — Injury, Matchup, DVOA, Volume, Pace, and more. You see one clear confidence score. Tap for the full breakdown of why each agent agrees or disagrees. No spreadsheets, no formulas — just a number you can trust.

AI-Built Pick Combos

Our engine builds optimized multi-pick entries for Pick6, Underdog, PrizePicks, and FanDuel. Choose from 2-pick safe plays to 6-pick moonshots — each one graded LOW, MEDIUM, or HIGH risk with clear reasoning.

Best Odds, Instantly

See real-time odds from every major sportsbook side by side. One tap shows you the best available line for any prop — so you always get the most value on every pick.

Build & Score Your Own Parlays

Have a combo in mind? Build your own custom parlay and let our AI engine score it instantly. See the combined confidence, correlation risk between legs, and whether the math actually supports the play — before you lock it in.

Self-Learning Engine

Every week, our system backtests every prediction against real results and automatically adjusts agent weights to maximize win rate. The engine literally gets smarter with every game played — no manual tuning, no stale models.
