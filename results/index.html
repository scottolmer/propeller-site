How grading works

No mystery math in the public record.

Propeller grades after games finish, compares the predicted side to the final stat value, keeps pushes separate, and reports the record without treating pushes as wins.

01

Prediction logged

The model records player, market, line, direction, confidence, sport, date, and book before the result is known.

02

Outcome graded

Final stat values are compared to the line. Overs win above the line, unders win below the line, and exact ties are pushes.

03

Record updated

Wins, losses, pushes, totals, sport splits, and win rate refresh from the public results API.
