Frequently Asked Questions

How much does Pick6 pay for a 4-pick entry?

A 4-pick Pick6 entry pays 10x your entry amount. A $10 entry returns $100 if all 4 picks hit.

What is the maximum Pick6 payout?

The maximum standard multiplier on Pick6 is 40x on a 6-pick entry. With a $100 entry, that's a $4,000 payout.

How does Pick6 compare to PrizePicks payouts?

Pick6 pays 40x on 6 picks vs PrizePicks' 25x. Pick6 has higher multipliers at 6 picks, but both platforms pay identically at 2–5 picks. PrizePicks offers Flex Play for partial payouts.

Does Pick6 have Flex Play?

Pick6 offers an Insurance mode on some entries that provides partial refunds if you miss one pick. However, the primary entry type is all-or-nothing with fixed multipliers.
