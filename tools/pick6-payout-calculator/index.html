How are DraftKings Pick6 payouts calculated?

DraftKings Pick6 is a peer-to-peer daily fantasy sports contest. For a prize-eligible entry, the minimum guaranteed payout is the entry amount multiplied by the Base Multiplier shown by DraftKings. Final contest winnings can be higher if the entry earns Extra Winnings.

What this calculator estimates

The minimum guaranteed payout and return above the entry amount using the multiplier you copy from DraftKings.

What it cannot know

Extra Winnings depend on the final prize pool, winning entries, and Standings Points, so they cannot be calculated in advance here.

Ties and voids

A result equal to its projection is void. A player who misses the sport-specific minimum-play requirement is also void. Voids can reduce the scored pick level.

Use the final entry screen

Pick Multipliers, limits, promotions, and combinations can change the Base Multiplier. The multiplier and terms shown by DraftKings at submission control.
