ROI formula

One unit risked on every priced settled pick.

A win at positive American odds earns odds ÷ 100 units. A win at negative odds earns 100 ÷ |odds| units. A loss is −1 unit; a push is 0 units. ROI is net units divided by the number of priced settled picks, multiplied by 100.

Voids and settled rows without an auditable price are excluded from ROI. The summary reports odds coverage so excluded pricing gaps stay visible. Captured recommendation fields are immutable; an existing settlement is never silently overwritten.
