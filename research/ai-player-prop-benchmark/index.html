General AI research protocol

Six checks before a generic assistant supports a research decision.

Exact question

Name the sport, player or event, market, source, and the exact line under review.

Fresh sources

Require the timestamp of every time-sensitive source and prioritize official availability information.

Line verification

Stop when the current line cannot be independently confirmed on the relevant platform.

Countercase

Ask for the strongest evidence on both sides and the facts that would invalidate each case.

Missing data

Separate sourced facts from inference and list conflicts instead of filling gaps with confident prose.

No-action outcome

Permit no action when the line, source freshness, or necessary context remains incomplete.

This is a disclosure checklist, not an empirical performance score. Propeller will publish dated, completed evidence before assigning comparative scores. Use the free AI Sports Betting Prompt Builder to apply the protocol to a specific question.
