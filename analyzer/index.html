Behind the Numbers

How the AI Prop Analyzer Works

Eight specialized agents run simultaneously on every player prop. Each contributes a weighted signal that combines into a single confidence score — telling you the direction and how strongly the data supports it.

Injury Impact

HIGHEST WEIGHT

Models direct and cascading roster effects when players are ruled out. Usage redistributes — this agent traces exactly who absorbs the minutes and volume.

Matchup Analysis

SIGNAL WEIGHT 2.0

Defense vs Position (DvP) differentials measure how opponents have defended that position group. A favorable matchup amplifies statistical upside; a tough one suppresses it.

Pace & Environment

SIGNAL WEIGHT 2.0

Combines home and away team pace ratings with the Vegas game total to project statistical environment. Fast-paced high-total games create fundamentally different volume floors.

No-Vig Market

SIGNAL WEIGHT 2.0

Strips sportsbook margin from both sides to find true implied probability. Cross-book disagreement and juice direction reveal where sharp money has moved since open.

Usage & Tracking

SIGNAL WEIGHT 2.0

Integrates advanced tracking data — touches, drives, paint touches, time of possession — alongside usage rate. Surfaces edges that box score averages miss entirely.

Minutes Trajectory

SIGNAL WEIGHT 2.0

Detects role changes, starting lineup promotions, and blowout risk. Minutes are the multiplier on every stat — knowing 38 vs 26 changes the entire prop evaluation.

Hit Rate & Trends

SIGNAL WEIGHT 1.5

Recency-weighted hit rate treats the last five games more heavily than the full-season sample. Hot and cold streaks are measured relative to each player's own baseline.

Ensemble Scoring

COMBINED OUTPUT

All agents contribute weighted scores via calibrated weighted average. Agents returning no signal are skipped entirely — a neutral reading never dilutes a strong directional signal.

Each score above 50 signals OVER; below 50 signals UNDER. The confidence percentage represents how strongly all agents agree on the direction — scaled so 50% is neutral and 100% is maximum conviction. Regression correction dampens overconfidence on small-sample statistics. Read the full methodology for complete agent weight tables by sport.
