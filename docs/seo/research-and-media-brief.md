# Propeller Picks Research and Media Brief

Last updated: 2026-07-13

## One-Sentence Description

Propeller Picks is an AI-assisted player-prop research workspace for web and mobile that combines sport-specific signals, inspectable reasoning, calculators, and a documented historical data archive.

## What Is Verifiable Today

- Product: web, iOS, and Android player-prop research workflows.
- Sports represented: NFL, NBA, NHL, MLB, soccer, and PGA.
- Public evidence: a dated historical database snapshot and a smaller ledger produced by current API collapse rules.
- Data limitation: the historical archive includes repeated analysis snapshots and legacy retrospective data.
- Claim limitation: the archive is not a uniquely published forward-test, ROI, profit, or future-performance claim.
- Accountability: Scott Olmer is identified as founder and owner of the site's core product and methodology documentation.

## Language To Avoid

Do not describe the historical row total as unique picks, published recommendations, verified bets, an independently audited win rate, or proof of profitability. Do not imply that a directional confidence score is a calibrated probability unless a source explicitly establishes that definition.

## Responsible Use

Propeller Picks is a research and analysis tool, not a sportsbook. It does not accept wagers. Model outputs are uncertain, and past results do not guarantee future performance.
