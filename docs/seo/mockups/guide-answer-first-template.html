How Many Picks Can You Play in a PrizePicks Power Play?

Updated June 10, 2026·Verified against current PrizePicks rules·9 min read
Direct Answer

A PrizePicks Power Play allows 2 to 6 picks, and every pick must hit to win. A 6-pick Power Play pays 37.5x. Flex Play also allows 2–6 picks but still pays if you miss one (or two on 5- and 6-pick entries) at reduced multipliers.

Should You Play Power Play or Flex Play?

Power Plays only make sense when every leg is genuinely high-confidence. The math favors Flex on most 4–6 pick entries because the partial payouts protect against the single miss that kills most cards.
