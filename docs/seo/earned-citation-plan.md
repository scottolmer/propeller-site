# Earned Citation Plan

Last updated: 2026-07-13

Propeller Picks should earn citations by publishing useful, accurately defined resources and giving relevant researchers or editors a clear reason to reference them. This is not a link-exchange or manufactured-mention program.

## Citation-Ready Assets

1. **Historical archive and definitions** — `/results/` is the canonical explanation for raw graded analysis rows, collapsed ledger entries, source APIs, and limitations.
2. **Machine-readable snapshot** — `/data/performance-snapshot.json` supplies a dated data dictionary, exact units, sources, and limitations.
3. **Player-prop research framework** — `/guides/how-to-analyze-player-props/` provides a reusable, six-signal research method.
4. **Answer-first Help center** — `/help/` supplies concise product, grading, sport, platform, and confidence-score answers.
5. **Free calculators** — the PrizePicks, Pick6, and Underdog calculators answer high-intent payout questions without requiring signup.

## Outreach Priorities

| Priority | Audience | Relevant asset | Legitimate reason to cite |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 1 | Sports-data and responsible-gambling researchers | Results archive and snapshot | Clear example of why observation counts and prospective recommendations must be separated. |
| 2 | DFS and pick'em education publishers | Calculators and Help articles | Free utility plus directly stated payout and rule caveats. |
| 3 | Sports analytics newsletters and podcasts | Research framework and methodology | Concrete explanation of multi-signal player-prop analysis and its limits. |
| 4 | Product and AI directories | Homepage, About, and Editorial Policy | Verifiable product description, named owner, supported sports, and web/mobile access. |

## Outreach Rules

- Contact only outlets for which the specific resource improves an existing article, guide, directory, or research project.
- Lead with the useful source or correction, not a generic request for a backlink.
- Disclose affiliation with Propeller Picks.
- Never offer fabricated quotes, reviews, credentials, traffic, or performance claims.
- Do not ask for dofollow treatment, reciprocal links, or undisclosed placement.
- Record declines and no-responses as honestly as successful citations.
