Questions worth asking

What an AI betting tool can—and cannot—do.

Use the research to understand the line. Do not confuse a score with certainty.

Can AI predict sports bets?

AI can organize signals and estimate a direction, but sports outcomes remain uncertain. Model confidence can be wrong, source data can be incomplete, and a market can move after analysis.

Is Propeller an AI picks site?

Propeller publishes research signals and directional analysis. It does not accept wagers, place bets, promise profit, or replace your judgment.

How can I verify the claims?

Use the public results archive for historical context, the forward publication record for newly captured rows, and the model card for definitions and limitations.
